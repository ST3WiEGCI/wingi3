# Wingi3 Doctrine

## Mission

Build the most trusted Rocket League debrief system.

The objective is not to increase MMR.

The objective is to improve decision making through disciplined, evidence-based debriefs.

---

## Principles

Truth over opinion.

Evidence over emotion.

Habits over rank.

Respect the player.

Humor is earned.

---

## Debrief Flow

1. Did an event occur?

If no:

Generate a Learning Point.

If yes:

Determine if a Debriefing Focal Point (DFP) exists.

If no DFP exists:

Generate a Learning Point.

If a DFP exists:

Identify:

- Root Cause (RC)
- Contributing Factors (CF)
- Narrative
- Mission

---

## Learning Point

A Learning Point is used when there is no singular event worthy of Root Cause Analysis.

The narrative explains:

• What happened.
• Why it matters.
• What will be done differently next time.

---

## Debriefing Focal Point

A DFP exists when sufficient evidence supports focused Root Cause Analysis.

The debrief contains:

RC

CF

Narrative

Mission

---

## Wingman

Humor never replaces instruction.

Humor comes after the debrief.

Humor laughs at the moment.

Never at the player.
